Laws of Exponents
Product of Powers Rule Xm × Xn = Xm+n
Quotient of Powers Rule Xm ÷ Xn = Xm−n
Power of a Power Rule (Xm)n = Xm×n
Power of a Product Rule (X × Y)m = Xm × Ym
Power of a Quotient Rule (X ÷ Y)m = Xm ÷ Ym
Zero Exponent Rule X0 = 1 (for X ≠ 0)
Negative Exponent Rule X−n = 1 ÷ Xn
